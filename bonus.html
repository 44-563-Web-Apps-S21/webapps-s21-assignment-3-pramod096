The Brown Deer

The Superpower of the Brown Deer is being the most destructive wildlife species. Crops like corn, peas, cotton, watermelon, soybeans and peanuts experience the most damage from brown deer.

  • Lair of Nemesis:
    1. Take the blue line from 14th Ave. and change to the gray line at Andrew Square. Get off at 83rd street.
    2. Once you are on 83rd street, go straight, past the River. Take the second left and continue going straight.
    3. You can find the Brown Deer across the Forest from Mahogany Trees.
